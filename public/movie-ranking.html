The Absolute Bangers of All Movies in My Opinion

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Insidious

Down-right terrifying movie, had me imagining a red-faced demon on my wall staring at me as I slept. (Watch at your own risk.)

A Silent Voice

One of the most eye-opening anime movies. A long emotional rollercoaster and a good perspective from the main charcter which was once a bully.

The Nun

Another horror movie that messed with my active imagination for a while. Couldn't help, but avert my eyes from it during some scenes.
